Some polar curves \(r=f(\theta)\)

Rose (3 petals) \(\quad r=\sin 3\theta\)

Rose (4 petals) \(\quad r=\sin 2\theta\)

Cardioid \(\quad r=1+\cos\theta\)

Limason (Inner Loop) \(\quad r=5\sin\theta+2\)
